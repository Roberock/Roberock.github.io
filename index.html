Project

Currently I am a postdoctoral Research Scholar involved in a collaborative research effort with the National Institute of Aerospace (NIA) and the Dynamic Systems and Control Branch at NASA Langley. My research interests primarily focus on the development of methods for distributionally robust reliance/reliability-based design optimization, generalized uncertainty quantification, and stochastic modelling of complex systems and critical infrastructures.

Academic Background

I received my PhD from the University of Liverpool while doing research at the Institute for Risk and Uncertainty + School of Engineering and my dissertation focused on developing robust computational frameworks for the resilience, vulnerability and reliability analysis of power grids and other interconnected safety-critical systems. Before my PhD i obtained a Masters of Research in Decision-Making Under Risk and Uncertainty from the University of Liverpool, UK and a Master in industrial engineering at the faculty of Enrgy Engineering at the University of Bologna, Italy.
